What Is RTP?

RTP is a theoretical long-term average. It's calculated over millions of spins by game developers and auditors like eCOGRA and iTech Labs. A 96% RTP doesn't mean you'll get 96% back in one session—short-term results vary wildly. Over time, the game tends toward its RTP. RTP vs Volatility (Variance)

RTP and volatility are different. RTP is the long-term return; volatility is how often and how big wins are. Low volatility: frequent small wins, steadier bankroll. High volatility: rare but larger wins, bigger swings. A 96% RTP high-volatility slot can feel brutal in short sessions but may hit big.
