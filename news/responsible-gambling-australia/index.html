What Is Responsible Gambling?

Responsible gambling is treating gambling as paid entertainment, not a way to make money. You set a budget, stick to it, and never chase losses. Setting Deposit and Loss Limits

Most reputable online casinos let you set daily, weekly, or monthly deposit limits in your account settings. Once set, you cannot exceed them until the period resets (or after a cooling-off period). Loss limits cap how much you can lose in a period. These tools help you stay within a budget. Set limits before you start playing, not when you're already in a session. Session Time Limits

Session time limits remind you to take breaks. After a set duration (e.g. 1 or 2 hours), you receive a pop-up and must confirm to continue. Regular breaks reduce the risk of impulsive decisions and help you stay aware of time and money spent. Use this feature alongside deposit limits for better control.

Self-Exclusion Options

Self-exclusion lets you block access to your account for a chosen period (e.g. 6 months, 1 year, or permanently). During this time, you cannot log in or place bets. Some operators participate in national self-exclusion schemes. If you feel you need a break, self-exclusion is a powerful tool. Recognising Problem Gambling

Warning signs include: gambling more than you can afford, chasing losses, lying about gambling, borrowing money to gamble, neglecting work or relationships, and feeling anxious or irritable when not gambling. If you recognise these in yourself or someone close to you, seek help. Problem gambling is treatable. Reality Checks and Game Design

Reality checks are periodic reminders (e.g. every 30 minutes) showing how long you've been playing and how much you've won or lost. They help you stay aware. Budgeting Before You Play

Decide how much you can afford to lose before logging in. Never use rent money, bills, or savings. Treat that amount as the cost of entertainment—once it's gone, stop. Gambling and Alcohol

Alcohol impairs judgement and can lead to larger bets and chasing losses. If you drink while gambling, set stricter limits and shorter sessions. Many players choose to gamble sober to stay in control.
